Return & Exchanges

Our Return Policy

At CycleShop, we prioritize customer satisfaction. If you're not happy with your purchase, you can return or exchange the item within 30 days of delivery.

Conditions:

  • - Items must be in their original condition, unused, and with all packaging intact.
  • - Proof of purchase is required for all returns and exchanges.

Return Process

  1. 1. Contact our customer support to initiate the return.
  2. 2. You will receive a return authorization and shipping label via email.
  3. 3. Package the item securely with the original receipt and return label.
  4. 4. Ship the item back to us. We will process your refund within 5-7 business days after receiving the return.

Exchange Process

  1. 1. Contact our customer support to arrange for an exchange.
  2. 2. Follow the same steps as the return process.
  3. 3. Once we receive your returned item, we will ship the new product.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our support team. We're here to help!
