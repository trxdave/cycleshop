Shipping Information

Free delivery on all orders over €500!

Domestic Shipping

We offer free domestic shipping on all orders over €500. For orders under €500, a flat shipping rate of €15 applies.

Delivery Time:

  • - Standard shipping: 3-5 business days
  • - Express shipping: 1-2 business days (additional €10 charge)

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and weight of the order.

Delivery Time:

  • - Standard shipping: 7-14 business days
  • - Express shipping: 3-7 business days

Note: Customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations. Customers are responsible for these fees.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, we will send you a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the courier's tracking page.

Additional Information

  • - Shipping times may vary due to public holidays or natural delays.
  • - For any shipping inquiries, feel free to contact us.
